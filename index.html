Our Mission

Our Mission Statement

At OffTheBattleFieldFoundation, our mission is to bridge the gap between veterans of war and compassionate companions worldwide, fostering bonds of understanding, support, and cultural exchange. Through our innovative programs, we strive to enhance the mental well-being of veterans by providing them with dedicated companionship while empowering students to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Veteran

If you're a veteran interested in cultural exchanges and eager to share your experiences with a Ukrainian-speaking student, we invite you to connect with us.

Volunteer

If you're a student keen on obtaining crisis and trauma intervention training and looking forward to being matched with Ukrainian veterans and refugees, we encourage you to connect with us.
